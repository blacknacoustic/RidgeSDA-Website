Meet Our Pastor

Dr Donald Burden

Dr. Donald L. Burden has pastored for 34 years. He served 31 of those years in the Allegheny West Conference in various capacities.

His favorite Bible text is Proverbs 3:5,6 “Trust in the Lord with all thy heart and lean not unto thy own understanding. In all thy ways acknowledge Him and He shall direct thy path.”

His motto for life is Jehovah Jireh- The Lord will provide; the Lord will see to it.
