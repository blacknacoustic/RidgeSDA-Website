Meet Our Pastor

Dr Donald Burden

Dr. Donald L. Burden has pastored for 34 years. He served 31 of those years in the Allegheny West Conference in various capacities.

His favorite Bible text is Proverbs 3:5-6: “Trust in the Lord with all thy heart and lean not unto thy own understanding. In all thy ways acknowledge Him and He shall direct thy path.”

His motto for life is "Jehovah Jireh- The Lord will provide; the Lord will see to it."

Secretary's Corner

Welcome to the Ridge Church community! We are delighted to share some important updates and highlights with you. Our goal is to keep everyone informed and engaged with the various activities, events, and initiatives happening within our church. Here are some key things you should know:

  • Welcome to the Ridge

    Welcome to the Ridge Church! We are thrilled to have you here and hope you find a warm and welcoming community. We encourage you to take part in our various ministries and activities to help grow our church and deepen your faith. Whether you’re looking to volunteer, join a group, or simply connect with others, there is a place for you here. Enjoy your time with us and we look forward to growing together!

  • Ridge Area Cashapp

    We are excited to offer the ability to give via Cashapp! Your generous contributions can now be made easily and securely through this convenient platform. Thank you for your support!

  • Ridge Community Center

    The Ridge Community Center is dedicated to helping those in need within our community. We distribute food to individuals and families to ensure everyone has access to nutritious meals. We welcome all community members to visit the center and benefit from our services.

    Mondays from 10 AM-1 PM and Fridays from 12-3 PM

  • Healthy Roots Garden

    Our Healthy Roots Garden is a wonderful place to connect with nature and grow fresh produce. We encourage everyone to participate, whether you are an experienced gardener or just getting started. Join us to learn about sustainable gardening practices and enjoy the fruits of your labor.

    Monday from 4:30 PM to 6:00 PM West Gate Entrance

  • Ridge Church Choir

    The Ridge Church Choir welcomes all singers to join. Whether you’re an experienced vocalist or a beginner, we invite you to come share your love for music with us. This is a wonderful opportunity to make new friends, enhance your singing skills, and participate in uplifting our church services through song.

    Every Thursday at 7 PM

  • Bulletin Announcements

    Please submit all bulletin announcements to the church secretary by Wednesday at 5 PM. Your timely submissions help us ensure that our weekly bulletin is comprehensive and up-to-date, keeping our congregation informed about upcoming events, important news, and special highlights within our church community. Thank you for your cooperation!
